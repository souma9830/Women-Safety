    Solutions for Sadness & Stress

    Guided Meditation

    • Find a comfortable place to sit or lie down.
    • Close your eyes and take a deep breath in... and out.
    • Focus on your breathing and let go of any tension.
    • Repeat a calming affirmation: "I am at peace"

    Understanding Mental Health

    Mental health affects how we think, feel, and behave. Common conditions include:

    • Anxiety Disorders: Excessive worry, panic attacks, phobias.
    • Depression: Persistent sadness, loss of energy, disinterest in activities.
    • Stress & Burnout: Feeling overwhelmed by daily life.
    • PTSD: Trauma-related distress and flashbacks.

    When to Seek Professional Help

    If distress persists, seeking professional help is important.
